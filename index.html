Mission Statement

Great coffee and collaborative working environments should never be difficult to find.

Coffee Collective has the solution for the student or professional in need of a communal work space. Our app streamlines the process of discovering cafes in Sacramento that empowers individuals to work from any location. Simply select a zip code, and we will connect you with the best local coffee shops
