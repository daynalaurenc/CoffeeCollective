Our Mission,

To connect enthusiasts with great coffee, ambiance, and collaborative work environments!

Have you ever spent 20 minutes texting back and forth, trying to narrow down a meeting place? Needing a coffee in hand, but forced to weigh out different factors such as distance, accommodations, and parking? Let us do the leg work for you!

Coffee Collective has the solution for the student or professional in need of a communal work space. Our app streamlines the process of discovering cafes in Sacramento. Simply select a zip code, and we will connect you with the best local coffee shops
